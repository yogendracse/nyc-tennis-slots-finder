      Central Park Reservations

      Back to all locations

      Central Park

      Reservation Courts: 6

      Phone: (212) 280-0205 or (212) 280-0206 ext. 4 and then ext. 1

      The Central Park Tennis Center offers the largest collection of public courts in the city, with twenty-six clay and four hard courts at 93rd Street near the West Drive.

      Hours: 6:30 a.m. to dusk

      Website

      Online Reservation Details

      • Advance reservations online cost $15 per court, per hour.
      • Advance reservations at Central Park can be made by season permit holders only.
      • Players cannot make reservations the same day of play.
      • Players can rebook or modify an existing reservation one time, however, online reservations are non-refundable and non-transferable. Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance.
      • Players with rained out reservations may rebook them at any time during the course of the permit season.
      • Players can have no more than one active reservation per day.
      • Full Details
