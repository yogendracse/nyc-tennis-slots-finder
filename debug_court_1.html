      Online Tennis Court Reservations

      Our Online Tennis Court Reservations system allows players to make online tennis reservations at several popular sites throughout New York City. A limited number of courts have been set aside for online reservations at these sites, and any courts that are not reserved using the online reservation system remain open to walk-ons with a full-season tennis permit or single-play tennis permit. Online reservations cost $15 per court, per hour.

      Reservations at Central Park can also be made in person or by phone. Visit our Tennis at Central Park and Prospect Park page to learn more more about tennis court reservations at Central Park and Prospect Park.

      Program Notes

      • McCarren Park walk-on courts contain six full-size courts, and two half-size courts for children.
      • Learn more about Prospect Park tennis court reservation.

      Key Program Details

      • Advanced reservations online cost $15 per court, per hour. All players need a full-season tennis permit or a single play permit. A single play permit can be purchased online for an additional $15.
      • Players can make advanced reservations online up to seven full calendar days in advance.
      • Players cannot make reservations the same day of play.
      • Players can rebook or modify an existing reservation one time, at least one day in advance of play. However, online reservations are non-refundable and non-transferable.
      • Players with rained out reservations may rebook them at any time during the course of the permit season.
      • Players can have no more than one active reservation per day.
      • Full Details

      These program details differ at Central Park. If you wish to reserve a court at Central Park, please read the Central Park rules for more.
